💡 Key Insights

Steam hosts over 88,000 games, making it one of the largest digital game marketplaces.
More than 56,000 developers have published games on Steam.
The average game price is approximately $7.52, indicating affordable pricing.
Windows is the dominant supported platform, while Mac and Linux have much lower support.
Game releases have increased significantly after 2015, reflecting continuous market growth.
